About

TUYU is a Japanese music unit formed on June 12, 2019. The group consists of five members: guitarist and composer Pusu, female vocalist Rei, keyboardist Miro, illustrator Omutatsu, and animator AzyuN, who creates the MVs. The band is famously known for its energetic, hard-rock melodies that contrast with the melancholic and dark themes of its lyrics

Why TUYU?

I've been an avid fan of TUYU since their early years. Their music has always resonated with me, and I wanted to create my first web project to pay homage to them. I hope that this project can serve as a tribute to their artistry and the impact they have had on my life and that you may also discover their music and appreciate it as much as I do. So keep watch for rainy days, user, and let music accompany you through the storm.
