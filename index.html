About

TUYU is a Japanese music unit formed in June 12, 2019. The group consists of 5 members: guitarist and composer Pusu, female vocalist Rei, keyboardist Miro, illustrator Omutatsu, and the animator AzyuN for their music videos. The band is famously known for their energetic and rock hard melodies that top the melancholic dark themes in the lyrics.
